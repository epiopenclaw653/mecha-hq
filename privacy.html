Mecha — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Mecha ("we," "our," or "us") operates a social media video content service for small businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Business name, contact information, and social media account details
  • Content submitted for video creation (photos, descriptions, preferences)
  • Instagram and Facebook account access tokens for publishing content on your behalf

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and publish video content to your social media accounts
  • Communicate with you about your content and our services
  • Improve the quality and relevance of content we produce
  • Comply with legal obligations

Instagram and Facebook Data

Our app connects to Instagram and Facebook via Meta's official APIs. We use this access solely to publish video content on your behalf. We do not sell, share, or use your Meta account data for any purpose other than content publishing. We request only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • instagram_content_publish — to post videos and images to your Instagram account
  • instagram_basic — to verify your account connection
  • pages_read_engagement — to confirm post status

Data Retention

We retain your account information and content for as long as you are an active client. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Access tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time through your Meta account settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to operate our service (e.g., cloud hosting providers) or as required by law.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All access tokens and credentials are stored in encrypted environments and are never shared or exposed publicly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke Instagram/Facebook access at any time via Meta's app settings
  • Opt out of our service at any time

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
